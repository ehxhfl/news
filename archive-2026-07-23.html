The photos taken by Curiosity showed an area nicknamed 'Jawbone Canyon.' The researchers analyzed that a powerful sandstorm here created a wave pattern by pushing the sand like a wave, and over time, this terrain hardened and turned into rock. In fact, a ripple-like structure that looks like waves is clearly visible on the surface of the rock in the photo. This image was taken in 2024, and the research results on this discovery were published in the international academic journal 'Geology' this year.
